## Six Key Messages

### 1. Frame the PoC Appropriately

> "This Proof-of-Concept provides order-of-magnitude estimates using the best available public data. It's designed for decision-making, not academic publication. We've been transparent about uncertainties."

### 2. Lead with Uncertainty

> "These are directional estimates with wide ranges, but they're consistently positive across all 32 scenarios we tested. Even under pessimistic assumptions, both interventions show positive returns."

### 3. Emphasize Robustness

> "The lowest RTE LNPV is Rs 3.9 lakhs. The lowest Apprenticeship is Rs 19.6 lakhs. Even if we're wrong by 40%, both programs remain worth doing."

### 4. Highlight the Decomposition Insight (KEY FINDING)

> "For RTE, approximately 80% of the economic benefit comes from improved formal sector entry (30% vs 9.1%), and only 20% from test score gains. This suggests RTE's primary value is as a pathway to formal employment, not just improved learning outcomes."

### 5. Break-Even Framing

> "We don't have your cost data yet, but we've calculated break-even thresholds. If RTE costs less than Rs 3-6 lakhs per beneficiary (depending on region), it achieves a 3:1 benefit-cost ratio. This lets you assess cost-effectiveness without sharing your financials."

### 6. Clear Next Steps

> "To upgrade this to a causal evaluation, we'd need longitudinal tracer data on beneficiaries—cost approximately Rs 5-8 lakhs—and 150-200 additional hours of work."

## Three Things We're Confident About

1. **Direction:** Both interventions generate positive lifetime returns. This is robust across all scenarios and sensitivity tests.

2. **Mechanism:** Formal sector employment is the key driver. The wage gap between formal and informal sectors compounds over a 40-year career from 2.25x to 3-4x.

3. **Regional patterns:** South/West urban returns are 50%+ higher than North/East rural. This reflects real labor market differences, not model artifacts.

---

## Three Things We're Less Certain About

1. **Magnitude:** Our point estimates could be off by 20-40% due to selection effects. The Monte Carlo range captures this.

2. **P_FORMAL_RTE:** Is 30% formal entry accurate? This is our #1 sensitivity driver. A tracer study would resolve it.

3. **Apprenticeship half-life:** Does the wage premium persist 5 years? 12? 30? This significantly affects Apprenticeship LNPV.
